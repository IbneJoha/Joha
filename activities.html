🧪 Research Activities
  • Graduate Research Assistant, Wireless Communications and Artificial Intelligence (WiComAI) Lab (September 2023 – Present), Kookmin University, Seoul, South Korea. Working on AI-driven IIoT, NILM, edge computing, and federated learning under Prof. Yeong Min Jang.
  • Solar PV Power Forecasting — Government & Industry Funded Project (2023–Present). Real-time one-day-ahead photovoltaic power generation forecasting across 10 solar sites using deep learning models. RMSE < 8%.
  • Real-Time Object Tracking System — Government Funded Project (2023–Present). Object detection and tracking combined with adaptive zoom and fuzzy logic gimbal control for UAV/UGV platforms (Jetson Nano, LattePanda).
  • AI-Driven Secure IIoT Energy Management (2023–Present). Developing secure IIoT frameworks with TCN-GRU-Attention models for real-time load forecasting and anomaly detection deployed on edge devices.
  • ARBGTU-Net-NILM (2024–Present). AI-based architecture for real-time Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring (NILM) in secure resource-constrained edge environments. Achieves 51.27% F1-score improvement over state-of-the-art. Validated on REDD and real-world datasets.
  • Federated Learning for Smart Energy Systems (2024–Present). Exploring FL for decentralized, privacy-preserving AI training across distributed edge nodes for load forecasting, disaggregation, and anomaly/intrusion detection.
