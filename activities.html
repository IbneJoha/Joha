🧪 Research Activities
  • Graduate Research Assistant, WiComAI Lab (September 2023 – Present), Kookmin University, Seoul, South Korea. Working on AI-driven IIoT, NILM, edge computing, and federated learning.
  • Solar PV Power Forecasting — Government & Industry Funded (2023–Present). Real-time one-day-ahead PV forecasting across 10 solar sites using deep learning. RMSE < 8%.
  • Real-Time Object Tracking System — Government Funded (2023–Present). Object detection and tracking with adaptive zoom and fuzzy logic gimbal control for UAV/UGV platforms.
  • AI-Driven Secure IIoT Energy Management (2023–Present). TCN-GRU-Attention models for real-time load forecasting and anomaly detection on edge devices with TLS/SSL security.
  • ARBGTU-Net-NILM (2024–Present). AI architecture for real-time NILM in secure edge environments. Achieves 51.27% F1-score improvement over state-of-the-art. Validated on REDD and real-world datasets.
  • Federated Learning for Smart Energy Systems (2024–Present). Privacy-preserving FL for load forecasting, disaggregation, and anomaly/intrusion detection across distributed edge nodes.
